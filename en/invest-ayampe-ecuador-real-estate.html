5 reasons to invest by the ocean in Ayampe

Updated July 2026 · By the Sandpiper Villas team, Ayampe

While other destinations on Ecuador's coast grew fast and messy, Ayampe chose to grow slowly and well. For an investor, that difference is everything. Here are five reasons more and more buyers — Ecuadorian and international — are looking at this corner of Manabí.

1. Real scarcity: a town that limits its own growth

Ayampe keeps its construction low-rise and its footprint small, surrounded by protected natural areas. That means supply limited by design: available land doesn't multiply, and what's built with care holds and gains value.

2. Growing vacation-rental demand

Surfers, families, digital nomads and wellness travelers arrive all year — peaking in the sunny season (December–May) and whale season (June–September). A well-managed home in Ayampe works four seasons, not just the holidays.

3. Entry prices still accessible

Compared with established Pacific destinations — Costa Rica, Mexico, or even other parts of Ecuador — the price per square meter in Ayampe remains accessible for what it delivers: front-row nature, 3 hours from Guayaquil, in a town with a protected identity. And Ecuador uses the US dollar, which removes currency risk for American buyers.

4. Access and infrastructure keep improving

The Ruta del Spondylus connects Ayampe with Puerto López, Montañita and the airports of Manta and Guayaquil. The town's services — fiber internet, shops, healthcare in Puerto López — have improved steadily without changing its character.

5. A community that protects the destination's value

The biggest risk for any beach destination is dying of success. Ayampe's community — devoted to sustainability, river conservation and sea-turtle protection — is the best long-term insurance for an investment here: what makes the town special is actively defended.

Frequently asked questions

Can foreigners buy property in Ecuador?

Yes. Ecuador allows foreigners to buy property with the same rights as citizens, and the country uses the US dollar, which simplifies the process for American and international buyers.

What returns can a vacation rental in Ayampe generate?

It depends on the property and its management. Ayampe receives travelers year-round — surf, whales, families and digital nomads — which supports occupancy across several seasons rather than just holidays. Ask for property-specific projections before deciding.
